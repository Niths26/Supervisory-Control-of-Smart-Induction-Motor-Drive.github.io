About The Project

In this project, we have designed an efficient closed-loop V/F speed control method which uses different control techniques like PI, Fuzzy tuned P, ANN and gives us the most efficacious method among them. Along with this, the system's smartness is increased by integrating the system with cloud. The data is then transfered to thingspeak and excel sheet.

The Prime Features of the project

  • 1

    Induction motor

    Induction Motors are widely used and most accepted motor in the industrial applications. This motor is also used for domestic and commercial applications.

  • 2

    V/F Speed Control Method

    Closed-Loop V/F Speed control technique is one of the most capable methods to run the machine expeditiously.

  • 3

    Use of Different Control Techniques for Drive

    Among the different closed-loop methods there different methods are applied. They are PI, Fuzzy tuned P and ANN.

  • 4

    Comparison of Different Cotrol Techniques

    Among the three chosen closed-loop techniques, the best one is selected comparing the results off the three methods.

  • 5

    Integration with Cloud

    Controling and Monitoring becomes much trouble-free when the system is integrated with the Cloud through platforms like Thingspeak.

  • 6

    Increased Smartness using App and Website

    New-gen technologies are integrated with the system to make it more coherent and systematic.
